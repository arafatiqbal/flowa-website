Features

Built around your whole self

Flowa brings together menstrual health and Islamic practice — because they've always been connected.

🌸

Period & Cycle Tracker

Log your cycle and get accurate predictions. See your current phase — menstrual, ovulatory, or low-fertility — with countdown to your next period.

🤲

Ghusl Reminder

When your period ends, Flowa gently reminds you to perform ghusl so you can return to salah and fasting with ease.

🕌

Prayer Tracker

Log your five daily prayers — Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha. On period days, Flowa shows an encouraging reminder that you are excused and beloved.

🌙

Qada Fast Tracker

Track missed Ramadan fasts by year and reason — menstruation, illness, travel. A visual progress bar shows how many you've made up.

📿

Dua Library

Over 100 authentic Islamic supplications organized into five categories: Morning Adhkar, Evening Adhkar, After Salah, Daily Duas, and Selected Duas — with Arabic, transliteration, and translation.

📊

Cycle Insights & Logs

Log your daily flow, symptoms (cramps, fatigue, bloating), and mood. View your cycle regularity and a 30-day history at a glance.

Five curated collections of authentic adhkar and duas — sourced from Quran and Sunnah, with Arabic, transliteration, and English translation.
